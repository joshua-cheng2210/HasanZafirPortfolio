Battery Cell Testing and Cooling System Improvement

This project focuses on testing and optimizing battery cells to enhance their thermal performance and longevity. The work involves evaluating the cooling system efficiency and making necessary improvements to manage heat generation during operation.

Advanced techniques, including CFD simulations and thermal imaging, are utilized to identify hot spots and design an improved cooling mechanism. This ensures better heat dissipation, leading to enhanced performance and safety of the battery cells.

The results from the testing phase are analyzed to refine the cooling system further, ensuring it meets the demanding requirements of high-performance electric vehicles like the Formula SAE car.
